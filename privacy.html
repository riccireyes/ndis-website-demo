Privacy Policy

Effective Date: April 2026

Prime Clinical Nursing PTY LTD ("we", "our", "us") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, disclose, and store your personal information in accordance with the Privacy Act 1988 (Cth) and the Australian Privacy Principles (APPs).

1. Information We Collect

We collect personal information that is reasonably necessary to provide our disability and clinical nursing supports. This may include:

  • Name, contact details, date of birth, and culturally specific needs.
  • Health and medical information, NDIS plan details, and clinical assessments.
  • Emergency contact and next of kin details.

2. How We Collect Information

We primarily collect information directly from you (e.g., during intake, via our contact form, or during service delivery). Where reasonable, we may collect information from a third party (such as your NDIS Support Coordinator, advocate, or medical professionals) provided you have given your consent.

3. Use and Disclosure

Your personal information is used exclusively to:

  • Provide safe, high-quality, person-centred clinical and community support.
  • Comply with NDIS Quality and Safeguards Commission reporting requirements.
  • Manage our administrative and operational activities.

We will not disclose your personal information to third parties without your explicit consent unless required or authorised by law (e.g., in a life-threatening emergency).

4. Data Security

We take all reasonable steps to protect your personal information from misuse, loss, unauthorised access, modification, or disclosure. Paper records are stored securely, and digital files are protected with passwords and encryption on secure, backed-out cloud servers.

5. Access and Correction

You have the right to access the personal information we hold about you and request corrections if it is inaccurate, incomplete, or out-of-date. Please contact our Privacy Officer to make a request.

6. Contact Us / Complaints

If you have any questions about this policy or wish to make a privacy complaint, please contact our Privacy Officer (Michelle Ballesteros) at info@primeclinicalnursing.com.au. Alternatively, you can escalate concerns to the Office of the Australian Information Commissioner (OAIC).
